FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Honest answers to the things that matter most.

Can Sole SPM help practice managers oversee the whole firm?
Yes. Sole SPM gives you a single end-to-end view of every client and engagement: status, ownership, deadlines, billing, and capacity. Partners and ops leads get a real-time helicopter view — and can drill into any cell.

Does Sole SPM support staff management and workload visibility?
Yes. Drag-and-drop weekly workload, a skill matrix per staff member (L1–L5), capacity forecasts by team and service line, and AI suggestions for the right person on the right job — all in one place.

Does Sole SPM include payments and client management?
Yes. Take payments inline at the billing step, send branded invoices, and manage every client interaction in one timeline. No extra subscriptions or third-party plugins.

What kind of brand customisation is available with Sole SPM?
White-label client portal under your own domain, your logo and colours, branded comms (email, SMS, signed documents). Most competitors charge extra for this — we include it on Scale and above.

Is Sole SPM for Australian practice management firms?
Yes, Sole SPM is designed for Australian firms and supports the operational workflow, client management research and accounting workflows local practices rely on.

Is Sole SPM suitable for growing accounting and bookkeeping firms?
Yes. Sole SPM is built for Australian accounting and bookkeeping firms, helping them improve visibility, create scalable operating models as they grow, and stay close to compliance.
