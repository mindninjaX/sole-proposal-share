Common questions

Questions firms ask before signing

Honest answers to the things that matter most.

Can I keep using Xero or MYOB for my clients' books?
Yes. Sole is the practice layer that sits above your bookkeeping software, not a replacement for it. We integrate deeply with Xero (including Xero Practice Manager), MYOB AccountRight and Essentials. Clients, contacts, and engagement data sync both ways. Your bookkeepers keep their workflow; you get a single practice view across the firm.

How does migration actually work?
A dedicated migration assistant is assigned to your practice from day one. They'll audit your current stack, map out what can move across (client and CRM data, templates, questionnaires, and master data typically migrate well), and identify what needs a manual transition plan. We run your old tools and Sole side-by-side — there's no forced cutover. The timeline is set together with your team; every practice is different and we don't make promises we can't keep. Talk to us about your specific setup and we'll give you a straight answer.

Is Sole compliant with the ATO's BAS, STP, and PAYG requirements?
Yes. Sole lodges BAS, IAS and TFN declarations via Standard Business Reporting (SBR2) directly to the ATO. STP Phase 2 is fully supported. PAYG instalments, super (via SuperStream), FBT and Div 7A are built into the workflow. We're a registered software provider on the ATO's digital service provider register, and our compliance team tracks every legislative change.

Can I white-label the client portal with my firm's brand?
Yes. You can white-label the client portal and the practice platform under your brand — custom domain (e.g. portal.yourfirm.com.au), your logo and colours, your email sender. Clients never see "Sole" anywhere. White-label is available as a branding add-on — speak to our team for current pricing and what's included.

What happens to my existing client data?
We migrate it for you. Your data is hosted in Australian data centres (AWS Sydney), encrypted at rest and in transit, and you own it — you can export everything at any time, and we keep nothing on cancellation. Sole is built to Australian Privacy Act standards and takes data security seriously. Ask our team about our current compliance posture if you have specific requirements.

Do you offer a free trial? Is there a contract?
30-day free trial on every plan, no credit card required. After that, we bill monthly — no annual lock-in. Cancel anytime, and we'll help you export your data. Annual pricing locks in for 12 months; cancel after the first month and we'll refund the rest.
