Roadmap · Delivered

Delivered roadmap items

You voted, we delivered — every feature below was prioritised by feedback from the Australian firms using Sole every day.

Accountants & Bookkeepers

4 shipped

  • Manage accountants & bookkeepers

    Allow users to view details of their accountant and bookkeeper, so the reports can be easily shared and approvals stay clean.

  • Accountant & Bookkeeper Listing

    Provide a shared list of accountants and bookkeepers for Sole to match if you need their assistance in any area.

  • Optional account sharing

    Granular permissions so a user can choose to share their books with their accountant — or revoke at any time.

  • Accountant & Bookkeeper Portal

    Branded practice space where partner firms manage multiple client accounts side by side, with a clean handover trail.

Advanced Financial Modules & Compliance

3 shipped

  • Multi-currency

    Support for multiple currencies on customer invoices, supplier bills, and foreign-currency payments — without manual conversions.

  • Bank feed · re-auth failed connectors

    Improved the ability for users to re-connect their bank feeds in the event of failures.

  • Bank feed enhancements · auto-match

    Sole now suggests rules to match the most-likely transaction to bank-feed activity, with a one-tap approve flow.

Business Management

5 shipped

  • Reporting suite 1.0

    Make users to extract, edit and email their accounting reports — P&L, balance sheet, GST summary — in a single click.

  • Reporting suite 2.0

    Make users to extract, edit and send all of their accounting reports — with custom date ranges, comparatives, and saved layouts.

  • GST global setting

    Add the ability for non-GST users to turn off GST and have screens, transactions and reports adjusted accordingly.

  • Transition from Sole to Sole

    Improve accounting platform migration from Sole to Sole, with explicit mapping and an import-history audit log.

  • Transition · Sole to Xero

    Allow users whose business outgrows Sole to easily migrate their accounting books to Xero — chart of accounts, balances, history.

Industry Presets, Products & Services

1 shipped

  • Pre-configured packages, categories and templates per industry

    Sole now offers per-industry chart of accounts and packaged services, so a tradie, café or sole-trader signs up to settings that already fit.

Invoices

5 shipped

  • Scheduled invoices & reminders

    Allow users to create forward-dated invoices that are released on a chosen date — and send polite reminders on their behalf.

  • Recurring invoices

    Allow users to create recurring invoices over time, that can be automated weekly, monthly or annually.

  • BCC invoice emails

    Allow users an option to receive a BCC email when invoices are issued, so a clean record lands back in their inbox.

  • Editable invoice numbers

    Allow flexibility for users to edit number prefixes, sequences and per-customer counters to suit their internal accounting practices.

  • Notifications for upcoming & overdue invoices

    Provide Sole users with notification of upcoming invoices and reminders for the same — and a digest sent to their customers.

Jobs, Time & Scheduling

2 shipped

  • Timesheet tracking

    Capture time spent results to your firm's chart of accounts and understand the costs of effort going into each engagement.

  • Job and project codes

    Tag invoices and expenses to specific jobs or projects so you can see what each piece of work is actually paying for.

Payments

2 shipped

  • Monthly instalment payments & direct debit integration

    Seamless integration for the GoCardless payment in Sole. Get paid on time, on autopilot.

  • Online payment integration

    Seamless integration for online payments in Sole — Stripe, GoCardless, Apple Pay and Google Pay enabled out of the box.

Payments, Invoicing & Customer Experience

1 shipped

  • Send invoices & quotes to multiple contacts

    Choose multiple recipients for any quote or invoice, so the right person at every customer signs and pays without a chase.
