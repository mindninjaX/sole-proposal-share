Common questions

Questions firms ask before signing

Honest answers to the things that matter most.

Can I keep using Xero or MYOB for my clients' books?
Yes. Sole is the practice layer that sits above your bookkeeping software, not a replacement for it. We integrate deeply with Xero (including Xero Practice Manager), MYOB AccountRight and Essentials. Clients, contacts, and engagement data sync both ways. Your bookkeepers keep their workflow; you get a single practice view across the firm.

How long does migration actually take?
For most firms: under a month. A dedicated migration specialist runs the project — they import clients, engagements, documents, and templates from your existing tools (Xero XPM, Karbon, FYI Elite, Senta, MYOB, TaxDome, Cone, spreadsheets), shadow your team for a couple of weeks, and then we go live. There's no big-bang cutover; we run side-by-side until you're ready.

Is Sole compliant with the ATO's BAS, STP, and PAYG requirements?
Yes. Sole lodges BAS, IAS and TFN declarations via Standard Business Reporting (SBR2) directly to the ATO. STP Phase 2 is fully supported. PAYG instalments, super (via SuperStream), FBT and Div 7A are built into the workflow. We're a registered software provider on the ATO's digital service provider register, and our compliance team tracks every legislative change.

Can I white-label the client portal with my firm's brand?
Yes. On the Scale and Optimise plans, you get a fully white-labelled client portal — custom domain (e.g. portal.yourfirm.com.au), your logo and colours, your email sender. Clients never see "Sole" anywhere. Most competitors charge extra for this; we include it.

What happens to my existing client data?
We migrate it for you. Your data is hosted in Australian data centres (AWS Sydney), encrypted at rest and in transit, and you own it — you can export everything at any time, and we keep nothing on cancellation. Sole is SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001 in progress, and Australian Privacy Act compliant.

Do you offer a free trial? Is there a contract?
14-day free trial on every plan, no credit card required. After that, we bill monthly — no annual lock-in. Cancel anytime, and we'll help you export your data. Early-adopter pricing (24 months locked in) is available for the first 50 firms each quarter.
