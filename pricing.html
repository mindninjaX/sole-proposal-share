How pricing works

A pricing model that grows with your practice — not against it

Three promises that hold from day one through your hundredth client.

Principle 01

One price. Your whole team.

A single monthly fee covers everyone in your practice — no per-seat counting, no mid-year surprises when you hire. Your bill reflects your client volume, not your headcount.

Predictable to the month
Principle 02

Simple and fair, end-to-end

One flat price per plan, every included module listed in plain English. All 13 platform surfaces on every tier. No "starter" lock-outs, no "talk to sales" tax on knowing what something costs.

Every feature priced upfront
Principle 03

Extend only when you need to

Add-ons sit on top of your plan — extra KYC checks, document storage, payroll, payment processing, premium support — added when you need them. The platform never gates you on the work itself.

Opt in, opt out, monthly
