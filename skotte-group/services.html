What we do

Our services

Class-leading strategic advisory and program delivery, across five connected practices.

Our services

Transform your organisation, program or project

Five practices, one team. Our multi-disciplinary expertise means practical, evidence-based solutions that drive measurable outcomes — and we move fluidly across these services to give clients fully integrated support.

01 / Our services

Portfolio, Program & Project Management

We provide hands-on expertise in the planning, governance and delivery of complex, time-constrained and often politically sensitive portfolios, programs and projects — mega, large or small.

  • Portfolio governance optimisation and reporting
  • Program and project assurance and governance frameworks
  • Strategic, delivery, assurance and enterprise PMO design and management
  • Turnaround and remediation support for distressed projects
  • Implementation design and delivery of public policy and funding programs
  • Project management services and support
02 / Our services

Commercial & Transaction Advice

Poachers turned gamekeepers. We are accountants, engineers and lawyers — supporting clients in the planning, execution and negotiation of major infrastructure, services and technology contracts to help you procure and negotiate best value.

  • Financial and commercial analysis and advice
  • Market testing
  • Commercial and procurement strategy and negotiation
  • Tender preparation and market engagement
  • Bid evaluation and negotiation
  • Document management
  • Contract reviews, performance reviews and remediation
03 / Our services

Strategy & Policy Advice

Always practical, always with an eye to sustainable implementation — and we understand the political overlay too. We help clients review, challenge and redesign policy frameworks, and design and implement strategies that are impactful, implementable and supported by rigorous analysis and stakeholder consultation.

  • Supply chain optimisation
  • Contestability frameworks
  • Risk-based operational governance and control frameworks
  • Asset management strategy
  • Procurement transformation
  • Operational improvement and performance management
04 / Our services

Investment Decisions & Data Insights

We use deep experience in business case development and data analytics to help clients make evidence-based investment decisions — across infrastructure, transport, health, supply chain, ICT and policy. We typically recruit and manage multi-disciplinary teams on our clients’ behalf to deliver fully integrated business case support, from the largest programs to the smallest decisions.

  • Business case delivery and advisory team management
  • Document drafting and consultation
  • Options assessment and comparative analysis
  • Data analytics support
  • Implementation design, scheduling and costing
  • Reporting, data consolidation and governance
05 / Our services

Technology-led Transformation & Change

Change agents who understand the technology that catalyses it. We bring expertise in technology strategy, business architecture design and systems implementation, with a specific emphasis on enabling operational transformation and change.

  • Implementation of large-scale enterprise systems
  • Business architecture and operating model design linked to ICT enablers
  • Procurement and commercial management of vendors for complex procurements, acquisitions and renewals
  • ICT strategy and operating models
  • Commissioning and contesting ICT commodity services
  • Solution partnerships that enhance business planning, data migration and archiving
